Welcome to my world

|

I'm a builder who designs, codes, and ships. At the USC Iovine and Young Academy I learned to work across design, engineering, and product, not because it sounds impressive, but because real problems don't respect those boundaries. Being fluent in all three means I can collaborate with anyone on a team, translate between them, and help everyone pull in the same direction. I've helped ship a company-wide product with McCarthy Holdings and Palantir, built and launched my own apps, and made physical products with my own hands.

The work I'm most proud of starts with a real person struggling with something real, and ends with something that genuinely helps. I believe the best products make people feel seen.

A live v1 teaches you more in a week than a perfect spec does in a month. So I ship, watch how real people actually use it, and fix in daylight.
